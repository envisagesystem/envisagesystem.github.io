Privacy Policy

App: Price Calculator
Last updated: 07 February 2026
No account • No tracking • No data collection

Overview

Price Calculator is a simple calculator app that helps you calculate the total price of an item in a store by using: Price per unit × Quantity/Weight.

We respect your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any information.

  • No name, email, phone number, or address is collected.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No device identifiers are collected.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are used.

Data Storage

The app does not use any database or backend server.

Any values you type (price or quantity) are used only for calculation on your device and are not saved.

Permissions

Price Calculator does not require any special permissions.

  • No camera access
  • No microphone access
  • No contacts access
  • No storage access
  • No location access

Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party services such as analytics, ads, or crash reporting tools.

Children’s Privacy

Price Calculator does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected, there is no risk of child data collection.
