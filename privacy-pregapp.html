Last updated: 01-12-2025

1. Introduction

Envisage Systems Inc ("we", "our", "us") operates the mobile application PregApp ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and protected when using PregApp.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect personal data you enter in the app, including:

  • Due date or Last Menstrual Period (LMP)
  • Pregnancy week details
  • Weight logs
  • Mood logs
  • Kick counter entries
  • Profile information such as email address
  • Any other pregnancy-related inputs you record

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, OS)
  • App usage statistics
  • Anonymous analytics data

2.3 Third-Party Services

PregApp uses the following services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Crashlytics, Analytics)

Firebase privacy details: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display personalized pregnancy tracking features
  • To store logs such as weight, mood, and kick tracking
  • To improve the app’s performance and user experience
  • To provide customer support
  • To enhance security and prevent unauthorized access

We do NOT sell your personal data.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase services. We implement industry-standard protections including encryption and secure authentication. However, no digital platform can guarantee 100% security.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data except:

  • With Firebase and other service providers used to run the app
  • When required by law or legal processes

We never sell your information to advertisers or marketers.

6. Data Retention

Your information is stored as long as your account remains active. You may request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Stop using the app at any time

For requests, contact us at: envisagesystemsinc@gmail.com

PregApp is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
